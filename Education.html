Education for Women

Women's Education

The education of women are mostly high, with their graduation rate is higher than males, and they are less likley to drop out of school. However, Males have a 1 in 8 chance of getting a scholarship (for physical activity/sports) and Females have a 1 in 10 chance. In Brown University, females had a little more than half of the acceptance rate of men. Additionally, among fast food workers, 51.2% of them are women compared to 43.5% which are men. And on top of that, 0.4 have Masters degrees, meaning that they have an education, but still are not accepted into higher paying jobs.
